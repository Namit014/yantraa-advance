## Core Description

Microsoft 365 is a subscription-based productivity suite that integrates AI assistance (Microsoft 365 Copilot) directly into its applications. It enables users to create, collaborate, and work across documents, presentations, and data.

## Included Applications

- **Word with Copilot** — Draft documents smarter and finish faster.
- **Excel with Copilot** — Unlock insights and make informed decisions.
- **PowerPoint with Copilot** — Turn ideas into polished presentations.
- **Outlook with Copilot** — Manage email and calendar to move work forward.

## AI / Copilot Features

- **Image Generation:** Users can describe what they want, and Copilot generates images for inspiration, storytelling, or polished headshots.
- **File Context:** Users can add files to prompts to provide context and ground AI responses in their content.
- **Slide Generation:** PowerPoint agent can build slides from notes, documents, or outlines.
- **Brainstorming Agents:** The Analyst agent helps plan and gather insights for meetings.
- **AI Activation:** AI features respond only when the user chooses to use them.

## Included Services

- OneDrive
- Microsoft Teams
- Outlook
- Copilot app

## Access & Plans

- **Free Tier:** Word, Excel, and PowerPoint are available for free in the browser with a Microsoft account.
- **Paid Subscription:** Additional features are available through paid plans; users can compare plans and upgrade.
- **Existing Subscribers:** Users with an active Microsoft 365 subscription (or work/school license) can sign in to download and install desktop apps.

## Platform Availability

- **Desktop:** Download available for Windows, providing a focused desktop experience integrating Copilot.
- **Mobile:** A mobile experience is available, designed for flexible access to work.
