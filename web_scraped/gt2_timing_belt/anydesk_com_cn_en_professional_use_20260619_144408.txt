# AnyDesk Professional Remote Desktop Software

## Professional Usage Definition

AnyDesk defines professional usage as any use or connection from, to within a professional environment or network.

## Typical Professional Use Cases

- **Remote Support** – Providing IT support for customers and clients, including Help Desk and Mobile Support.
- **Remote Work** – Working from home, on the go, or in a hybrid setup.
- **Remote Access** – Managing multiple devices, maintaining and monitoring computers, or controlling high-performance machines in a business environment across all platforms.

## Personal Use

AnyDesk offers free remote desktop software for personal use, including all basic features. Personal users can help friends and family with technical issues or collaborate on projects from any location.

## Licensing

- Professional users are required to purchase a license.
- Existing license holders need to link their license to their client.
- Personal users who are wrongly classified as professional can fill out a form to get whitelisted.
