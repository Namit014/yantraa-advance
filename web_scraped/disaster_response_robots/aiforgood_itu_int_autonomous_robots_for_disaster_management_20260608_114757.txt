**Autonomous Robots**

* Equipped with artificial intelligence and advanced sensors
* Can navigate through complex and hazardous environments
* Can gather data and perform specific tasks with minimal human intervention
* Valuable assets in disaster response scenarios where human access may be limited or dangerous

**Drones**

* Used in disaster response scenarios, such as after Hurricane Katrina in the United States
* Faces challenges due to a top-down, policy-driven approach, necessitating coordination among local agencies
* Can be controlled by a single human through natural body motions
* Potential applications:
	+ Maneuvering through buildings and confined spaces
	+ Establishing communication networks in areas where traditional infrastructure is lacking
	+ Inspecting and collecting data
