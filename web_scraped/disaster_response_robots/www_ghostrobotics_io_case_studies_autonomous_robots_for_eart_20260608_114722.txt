**Solution**

* Purpose: Support relief operations in Noto Peninsula, Japan
* Deployed: Quadrupedal unmanned ground vehicles (Q-UGVs)
* Capabilities:
	+ Delivering small payloads (e.g., medical supplies, food, and water)
	+ Performing reconnaissance in unstable or high-risk areas
	+ Assisting with communications in areas with downed power and cell infrastructure
	+ Four-legged mobility allows climbing rubble, descending stairwells, and navigating uneven terrain

**Results**

* Enabled real-time assessment of collapsed structures and isolated zones without endangering personnel
* Delivered essential supplies to communities and aid teams in areas inaccessible to trucks or drones
* Demonstrated Q-UGV's capability as a low-footprint, all-terrain responder in complex disaster relief scenarios
* Validated the use of robotic systems in humanitarian missions
