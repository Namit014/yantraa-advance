The article discusses servomotors as precise control systems integrating motors, sensors, and controllers to achieve targeted motion. Variants include brushless AC power solutions and industrial gearheads, optimizing performance across applications. Reliable references confirm standard configurations under NEMA ICS 16 flange mountings. Feedback mechanisms via encoders enhance accuracy, though alternatives exist. System adjustments, such as variable-speed drives, refine control efficiency. Comparative analysis highlights trade-offs between open-loop and advanced implementations. Additional sources validate adoption in robotics and manufacturing contexts.
