**Delta Robot Design and Simulation**

* Delta parallel robot definition: a type of parallel robot with three arms connected to universal joints at the base, using parallelograms in the arms to maintain the orientation of the end effector.
* Components: Fixed Frame (Base), Proximal Link (Active Link), Distal Link (Passive Link), Spherical/ball joints, Revolute Joints, Moving Platform (end-effector)

**Kinematics Study**

* Inverse Kinematics: solving for joint angles (theta1, theta2, theta3) given end effector coordinates (x0, y0, z0)
* Forward Kinematics: solving for end effector coordinates (x0, y0, z0) given joint angles (theta1, theta2, theta3)
* Workspace: the total volume swept out by the tool center point located on the mobile platform, as the robot executes all possible motions
