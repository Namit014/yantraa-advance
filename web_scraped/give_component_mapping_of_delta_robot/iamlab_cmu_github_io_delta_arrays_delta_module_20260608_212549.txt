**Assembly Instructions:**

* Attach 3 linear actuators to 4 star-shaped mounts using 8-32 screws.
* Insert 12 actuators into square-shaped holes of 2 Plexiglass delta robot holders.
* Attach 1-½” Long spacers between star-shaped mounts and lower plexiglass holders.
* Attach 3-½” Long spacers between 2 plexiglass holders.
* Attach delta links to linear actuators.
* Attach Fingertip Clip Holders to end effector plates of delta robots and super glue Fingertips on top.

**Electronics:**

* Modifying the delta shield to produce PCB for distributing power to all motors.
* Attaching ADC and motor drivers to single shield design.
* Shorting all VCC and GND pins manually to achieve the final result.
