Thedocumentary follows the seven‑month development of Humanoid’s HMND 01 Alpha, the United Kingdom’s first industrial humanoid robot. The project engaged 170 specialists working across three development hubs—London, Boston and Vancouver—and was completed in a record seven‑month period. The robot is intended for logistics, retail and industrial applications, with a mission to be reliable, safe and helpful while augmenting rather than replacing human workers. Founder Artem Sokolov set the company’s goal to empower humanity through such robots. The film uses candid interviews and behind‑the‑scenes footage to reveal the real‑world challenges and breakthroughs of the engineering team.
