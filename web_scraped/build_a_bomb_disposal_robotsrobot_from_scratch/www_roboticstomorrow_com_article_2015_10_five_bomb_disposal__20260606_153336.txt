Here are the technical specifications and facts extracted from the article:

**Dragon Runner**

* Lightweight, back-packable, multi-terrain robot
* Capable of detecting a variety of devices without putting the operator in harm's way
* Configured with a manipulator arm for digging and moving objects
* Ability to place small charges to disrupt suspect devices
* Send video footage back to the operator at a safe distance
* Cross-country capability with twin tracks

**MarkV-A1**

* 800 pounds, about the size of a riding lawn mower
* 8 feet tall when manipulator arm is fully extended
* Tread system for traversing difficult terrain
* Can be armed with high pressure water disruptors or a shotgun
* Four color video cameras, microphones, lights, and sensors
* Height: 8 feet

**Daksh**

* Battery-operated remote-controlled robot on wheels
* Primary role is to recover bombs
* Can climb staircases, negotiate steep slopes, and navigate narrow corridors
* Can lift suspect objects and scan them using a portable X-Ray device
* Can defuse bombs with a water jet disrupter
* Shotgun for breaking open locked doors
* Can scan cars for explosives
* Can be remotely controlled over a range of 500 m in line of sight or within buildings
* 90% of the robot's components are indigenous

**iRobot 510 PackBot**

* Modular, adaptable, and expandable
* Can perform bomb disposal, surveillance, and reconnaissance, CBRN detection, and HazMat handling operations
* Can climb stairs and navigate narrow passages with sure-footed efficiency
* Relays real-time video, audio, and sensor data
* Operator can stay at a safer, standoff distance

**BDRP (Robo Sally)**

* Equipped with artificial limbs designed for amputees
* Has two cameras for a head and prosthetic arms
* Motors around on four wheels to tackle uneven terrain
* Has undergone changes to its electronics and platform since its first unveiling in 2008

**US Digital's L2 Low Profile Motor Encoder**

* Uses US Digital's proven EM1 optical encoder module and disk
* Has a 0.75 in. bolt-spacing mount
* Maximum resolution is 1250 CPR (5000 PPR with quadrature)
* Single-ended output
* Fast installation with a cover that snaps onto the base
* Manufactured in Vancouver, Washington, USA
