**T4 Bomb Disposal Robot**

* Designed for operations in crowded and difficult environments like subways, airports, and even hospitals
* Features:
	+ Sturdy all-terrain treads
	+ Movable manipulation arm with haptic feedback
	+ Camera system for enhanced visibility
	+ Skid steer track system for mobility
	+ Ability to climb stairs and traverse steep slopes
* Specifications:
	+ Runtime: over 7 hours with 6 BB-2590 batteries
	+ Line-Of-Sight (LOS) range: over 3300 ft
	+ Non-LOS range: exceeding 660 ft
	+ Operating temperature range: -4°F to +140°F
	+ Electromagnetic interference-shielded
	+ Maximum fording depth: 12 inches
	+ Sealed with IP66 for durability in various environmental conditions
* Weight: approximately 220 pounds (100 kilograms)

**T4 Robot Capabilities**

* Can fit through short aisles in buses, trains, and airplanes
* Can climb stairs
* Can unzzip bags and open glove boxes with ease
* Replaces older fleet of large EOD robots with T7 systems
* Features advanced haptic feedback, reducing training demands and enhancing mission efficiency

**Contract**

* The British Army has placed a £21 million deal with L3Harris Technologies
* The contract includes:
	+ Provision of 50 T4 EOD robots
	+ In-country support
	+ Maintenance
	+ Training
* The robots will be used by the 29 EOD & Search Group and the Diving Threat and Exploitation Group, Royal Gibraltar Regiment (RG) and Cyprus Operational Support Unit (COSU)
