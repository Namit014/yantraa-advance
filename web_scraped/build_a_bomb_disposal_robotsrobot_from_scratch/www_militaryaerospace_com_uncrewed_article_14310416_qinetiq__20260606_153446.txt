**MK 2 Man Transportable Robotic System (MTRS)**

* Provides stand-off capability to detect, identify, and dispose of IEDs and related hazards using unmanned ground vehicles equipped with special IED-disposal payloads.
* Can locate, identify, and clear land mines, unexploded ordnance, and IEDs in the path of maneuvering Army or Joint forces.

**Talon System**

* Lightweight vehicles designed for IED and explosive ordnance disposal, reconnaissance, communications, countering CBRNE threats, security, heavy lift, defense, and rescue missions.
* Can move as fast as 6 miles per hour.
* Transportable by one person.
* Works in bad weather and rugged terrain.
* High payload capacity and payload-to-weight ratios.

**Talon V**

* 16 I/O ports, including Interoperability Profile (IOP) A and B connectors.
* JAUS AS4-compliant software.
* Supports plug and play discovery of IOP devices.
* Offers a variety of high-definition and standard-definition camera options, including an optional, dual-purpose Thermal/Daytime zoom camera.
* Heavy-lift multiple-degree-of-freedom arm manipulator.
* Supports a variety of third-party and legacy Talon manipulators.

**Order Details**

* $11 million order to QinetiQ North America for option year three of production, engineering support, and post-production support of the MK 2 MTRS and Talon systems.
* Production of systems, depot-level repair parts, initial spares kits, consumables, repair parts, and approved accessories.
* Work to be done at locations to be determined on individual task orders, with completion expected by March 2026.
