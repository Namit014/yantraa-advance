**Arduino UNO® R4 Minima – Key Technical Specs**

- **Microcontroller**: Renesas RA4M1 (Arm Cortex‑M4)
- **Core Clock**: 48 MHz  
- **Flash Memory**: 256 kB  
- **SRAM**: 32 kB  
- **Operating Voltage**: 5 V (same as UNO R3)
- **Input Voltage (VIN)**: 6 V – 24 V DC (24 V tolerance)
- **Digital I/O Pins**: 14  
- **Analog Input Pins**: 6 (12‑bit ADC)  
- **DAC Output**: 1 × 12‑bit DAC  
- **PWM Channels**: 6  
- **Peripherals**:
  - UART: 1  
  - I²C: 1  
  - SPI: 1  
  - CAN BUS: 1 (with built‑in CAN transceiver)
- **Current per I/O Pin**: 8 mA  
- **USB Interface**: USB‑C® (for programming and HID output)  
- **Dimensions**: 68.85 mm × 53.34 mm  
- **Conformities**: UKCA, FCC, REACH, CE, RoHS, RCM, ISED/IC  

**Additional Features**

- Full compatibility with Arduino UNO R3 shields and accessories (same form factor, pinout, and 5 V logic).  
- Built‑in SWD (Serial Wire Debug) connector for debugging the Cortex‑M4 core.  
- Native HID support: can emulate mouse or keyboard when connected via USB‑C.  
- Expanded peripheral set (DAC, CAN, OP‑AMP) for more complex projects.  

These specifications provide the essential technical details needed to evaluate the UNO R4 Minima for design or development work.
