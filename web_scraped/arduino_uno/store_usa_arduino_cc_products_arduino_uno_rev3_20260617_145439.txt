**Arduino Uno Rev3 – Technical Specifications**

- **Microcontroller**: ATmega328P  
- **Operating Voltage**: 5 V  
- **Input Voltage (recommended)**: 7 – 12 V  
- **Input Voltage (maximum)**: 6 – 20 V  
- **Digital I/O Pins**: 14 (6 capable of PWM output)  
- **PWM Digital I/O Pins**: 6  
- **Analog Input Pins**: 6  
- **Maximum DC Current per I/O Pin**: 20 mA  
- **Maximum DC Current for 3.3 V Pin**: 50 mA  
- **Flash Memory**: 32 KB (0.5 KB used by bootloader)  
- **SRAM**: 2 KB  
- **EEPROM**: 1 KB  
- **Clock Speed**: 16 MHz (CSTCE16M0V53‑R0 resonator)  
- **Built‑in LED (LED_BUILTIN)**: Pin 13  
- **Dimensions**: 68.6 mm × 53.4 mm  
- **Weight**: 25 g (0.05 kg)  

**Key Features**

- USB connection, power jack (2.1 mm center‑positive) and battery terminals (GND, Vin).  
- ICSP header for in‑circuit programming.  
- Reset button and a resettable polyfuse (protects USB port from shorts/over‑current; trips at >500 mA).  
- Powered automatically from USB or external supply (6 – 20 V).  
- External supply recommended: 7 – 12 V (lower may cause instability; higher risks regulator overheating).  
- 5 V regulated output via the 5 V pin; 3.3 V regulated output via the 3V3 pin (max 50 mA).  
- IOREF pin provides the voltage reference for accessories/shields.  

These consolidated figures capture the essential electrical, mechanical, and power characteristics of the Arduino Uno Rev3 board.
