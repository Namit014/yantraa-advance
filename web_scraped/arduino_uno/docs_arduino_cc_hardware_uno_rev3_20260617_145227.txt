**Arduino UNO R3**

- **Microcontroller:** ATmega328P  
  - 16 MHz ceramic resonator  
  - 1 KB EEPROM (preserved when powered off)  
  - Not soldered to the board, so the chip is replaceable

- **Digital I/O:** 14 pins (6 capable of PWM output)  
- **Analog inputs:** 6 channels  
- **Power options:**  
  - USB serial interface for programming and power  
  - Barrel jack (standard 9 V battery or AC‑to‑DC adapter)  
- **On‑board features:** ICSP header, reset button  
- **Connectivity:** USB, power jack, barrel plug (fits a 9 V battery)  

The UNO R3 provides all hardware needed to run and program an ATmega328P, making it an ideal starter board for electronics and coding projects.
