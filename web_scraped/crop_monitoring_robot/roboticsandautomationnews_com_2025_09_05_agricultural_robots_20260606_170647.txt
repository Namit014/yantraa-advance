**Precision Spraying:**

* John Deere's See & Spray technology reduces herbicide usage by an average 59 percent across corn, soybean, and cotton operations
* 1 million acres were treated with See & Spray in 2024, yielding the same average savings of 3-4 bushels per acre

**Other High-ROI Use Cases:**

* Laser weeding: up to 80 percent reduction in herbicide use, 1-2 seasons
* Robotic harvesting: up to 50 percent cut in seasonal labor costs, operates 24/7
* Drone scouting: saves $10-$15/acre in scouting costs, enables targeted inputs
* Smart irrigation: 20-30 percent water savings, less manual labor

**Robotic Harvesting:**

* A strawberry-picking robot can harvest a 25-acre field in just three days, replacing a crew of around 30 workers
* For apples, current robots pick at a pace of one fruit every 5-10 seconds, compared to humans who usually manage one per second
* Pruning robots are under development to address costs, given that pruning can account for up to 25 percent of labor in fruit/vine operations

**Autonomous Tractors and Mobility Platforms:**

* John Deere's second-generation fully autonomous tractors, orchard sprayers, and remote dump truck "Dusty" are designed to run without drivers using advanced camera, LiDAR, and AI systems
* Farmers have already deployed first-gen autonomous tractors since 2022 for planting prep
* Deere aims for fully autonomous corn and soybean systems by 2030

**Broader Efficiency Gains via Digital Agriculture:**

* Precision agriculture can boost yields by 9-13 percent, while variable-rate irrigation can save ~25 percent water without hurting output
* Automated livestock systems, like milking robots, and greenhouse automation reduce labor and improve consistency
