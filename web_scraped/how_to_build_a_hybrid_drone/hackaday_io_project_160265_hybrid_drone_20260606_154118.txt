**Hybrid Drone Technical Specifications**

* **PlutoX Drone**: Modularity, allowing for easy addition of motor and wheel support, and using 400 rpm brushed motors and 600 mAh batteries.
* **3D Printed Wheels and Motor Support**: Designed to hold wheels and motor for rover mode.
* **Cygnus IDE**: Software development environment.
* **Pluto App**: Control software.
* **AutoStabilization**: Uses magnetometer data to maintain straight direction.
* **Error Correction**: RC input performs correction.
* **Motor Direction**: Forward (M2 and M3), Reverse (M2 and M3), Right (M2 forward, M3 reverse), Left (M2 reverse, M3 forward).
* **Auto-Stabilization**: Uses data from drone's magnetometer and yaw angle to move rover in straight line.

**Project Details**

* The objective is to create a hybrid drone that can fly and walk on the ground.
* The project uses a PlutoX controller, with an add-on breakout board to enable rover mode.
* The drone is controlled using a phone (controller).
* The project aims to bring together drone and rover capabilities, with the help of simple coding.

**Components**

1. PlutoX Connectors and Accessories
