**Hybrid Drone Systems**

* Powered by a compact gasoline-fueled generator
* Combines two single-cylinder, two-stroke engines with a timing belt to keep them 180 degrees out of phase
* Uses brushless motors as generators, combined with rectifier diodes and capacitors to smooth the voltage output
* Intended to be used in parallel with a lithium battery pack to ensure power availability in case of a temporary malfunction

**Goals and Expectations**

* Aiming to achieve two hours of flight time
* Seeking to massively expand the range of operation for medium to large multirotors
* To unlock capabilities limited by short battery runtimes

**Technical Challenges**

* Engine starting
* Vibration
* Rectification

**Experimentation and Testing**

* Ongoing experiments with 500 W, 1,000 W, and 2,000 W designs
* Work ongoing to optimize the engine and electronics package
* Flight testing has commenced
