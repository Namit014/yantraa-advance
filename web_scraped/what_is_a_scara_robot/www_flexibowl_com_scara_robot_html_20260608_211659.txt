**Scara Robot Technical Specifications:**

* Type: Industrial robot
* Configuration: Four-axis SCARA robot
* Description: Manipulator with four degrees of freedom, suitable for pick-and-place or assembly operations
* Advantages:
	+ Operate at higher speed
	+ Cleanroom specification available
	+ Achieve tolerances lower than 10 microns
	+ Compact layout, easily re-located in temporary or remote applications
* Limitations:
	+ Typically only capable of carrying a lighter payload (up to 2 kg nominal, 10 kg maximum)
	+ Limited dexterity and flexibility compared to full 3D capability of other types of robots
* Kinematics: SCARA arm has a strong mechanical structure, capable of withstanding unexpected stress and collisions
* Motion: Four degrees of freedom, with motion in X-Y and Z planes, and 360-degree rotational movement about the Z-axis
* Control: Inverse kinematics and data interpolation allow for dynamic, quick, and intelligent motion
* Applications: Assembly operations, including pick-and-place, where high speed and high accuracy are required
* Industries: Automotive, electronics, and other industrial fields where manufacturers need to feed bulk components of all sizes
