**What is a SCARA robot?**

* A type of robotic arm with several joints that give it flexibility to perform various tasks.
* Designed to mimic the movements of a human arm, but with unwavering precision and efficiency.

**Key Features:**

* Controller
* Power supply
* End-effector (gripper or special tool)
* Specialized software
* Unique range of motion, particularly in the X-Y plane
* Ability to move horizontally in any direction within its workspace
* Ability to move vertically, with limitations (vertical axis remains fixed)

**Capabilities:**

* Material handling operations, such as:
	+ Picking up and placing objects
	+ Transporting items
	+ Conducting assembly work
* Assembly and manufacturing tasks
* Pick-and-place tasks, with high speed and precision
* Dispensing tasks, such as applying adhesives, painting, and sealing

**Benefits:**

* High precision
* High speed
* Compact size
* Cost-efficient
* Easy integration with existing automated systems

**Applications:**

* Food and beverage industry
* Pharmaceuticals industry
* Microelectronics industry
* 3D printing (small-scale applications)
* Packaging and palletizing
* Dispensing (applying adhesives, painting, and sealing)

**Selection Criteria:**

* Application (purpose of the SCARA robot)
* Budget (payload capacity, speed, brand, and customization options influence the cost)
* Maintenance costs and availability of spare parts
