**SCARA Robots**

* Type: Serial Kinematics, 3 rotary axes and 1 linear axis
* Degrees of freedom: 4
* Compact footprint, robustness, and cost-effectiveness compared to models with multiple axes
* High repeatability and long service life
* Capable of performing pick-and-place tasks, gluing, joining, and assembly operations

**Construction**

* Fixed base with a rotating swivel arm as the first axis
* Rest of kinematic chain connected to this arm via joint (second axis)
* Integrated vertical and rotatable Z-axis (third axis)
* End effector, such as a gripper, can be attached here for additional linear motion (fourth axis)
