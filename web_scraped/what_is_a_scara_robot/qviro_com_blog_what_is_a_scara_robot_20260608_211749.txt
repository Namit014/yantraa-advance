* SCARA robot definition: A type of robot that can move its arm in different ways, similar to a human arm, with four parts that can move in X-direction, Y-direction, Z-direction, and rotation around the Z-axis.
* SCARA robot features:
	+ Fast and precise
	+ Can handle different tasks
	+ Can move its arm in different ways
	+ Can adjust its height (Z-direction)
	+ Can rotate around its vertical axis (Z-axis)
	+ Can do jobs that require a delicate touch
* Applications of SCARA robots:
	+ Making electronics
	+ Making cars
	+ Making metal products
	+ Making plastic items
	+ Making food
	+ Making medicines
	+ Making cosmetics
* Advantages of SCARA robots:
	+ Speed and precision
	+ Compact size
	+ Cost-effectiveness
	+ Can fit into existing systems
	+ Can learn new tasks
* Disadvantages of SCARA robots:
	+ Limited to lifting heavy objects (0.5 kg to 20 kg)
	+ Not suitable for large spaces

	+ SCARA robots used in precision engineering and optics
	+ Robots come in different groups based on their load capacity
