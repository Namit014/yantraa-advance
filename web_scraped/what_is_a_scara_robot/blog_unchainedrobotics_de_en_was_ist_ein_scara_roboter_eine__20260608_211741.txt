**What is a SCARA robot?**

* A type of industrial robot used in manufacturing and assembly processes
* Equipped with a horizontal arm that moves in two directions, allowing it to reach and manipulate objects within a limited working area
* Known for high precision and speed, making them well-suited for tasks that require precise movements or handling of small parts

**How do SCARA robots differ from other types of robots?**

* Constructed differently, with a limited range of motion in the vertical plane
* Typically smaller and more compact compared to 6-axis robots
* Faster and more precise than 6-axis robots, making them suitable for tasks that require high-speed assembly or handling of small parts

**Advantages of SCARA robots**

* Generally less expensive than other industrial robots, such as 6-axis robots
* Faster and more accurate, making them well-suited for tasks that require precise and repeatable movements
* Often used in small parts assembly lines, where they can perform tiny tasks quickly and accurately, improving overall efficiency and reducing the risk of errors

**Common processes automated with SCARA robots**

* Assembly: precise assembly of small parts, fastening screws, inserting pins, or assembling small components
* Pick and place: picking and placing small parts or components with a high degree of precision
* Packaging: packaging products for shipping or storage
* Dispensing: dispensing small quantities of liquids or other materials with high accuracy
