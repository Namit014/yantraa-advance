**Robot Dimensions:**

*   Base radius (f): mm
*   Bicep length (rf): mm
*   Forearm length (re): mm
*   End Effector radius (e): mm
*   Base to floor distance (b): mm

**Steps per Turn:**

*   Motor precision is defined by steps per turn.
*   1.8-degree steppers have 200 steps per turn.
*   With 1/16th microstepping, this equates to 3200 steps per turn.

**Envelope Calculations:**

*   **Rectangular Cuboid Envelope:** Defines the reachable workspace in a box shape.
*   **Motor Angle Limits:** Specifies the required motor rotations to traverse the cuboid envelope.
*   **Center:** Location of the envelope's center relative to the base (0,0,0).
*   **Home:** Tool position when all arms are parallel to the floor.
*   **Resolution:** Measures the precision of movement.

**Kinematics:**

*   **Forward Kinematics:** Calculates the XYZ position based on motor angles.
*   **Inverse Kinematics:** Calculates motor angles based on the XYZ position.
*   Zero degrees for motor angles represents the bicep being horizontal to the floor.

**Input Parameters:**

*   Motor 1 degrees
*   Motor 2 degrees
*   Motor 3 degrees
*   X mm
*   Y mm
*   Z mm
