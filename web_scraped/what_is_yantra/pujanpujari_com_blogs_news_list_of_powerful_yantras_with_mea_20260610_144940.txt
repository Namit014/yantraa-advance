**What is a Yantra?**

* A Yantra is a machine used to worship Navagrahas, elements, gods, and goddesses in homes or at temples.
* Each deity has a specific Yantra.

**Types of Yantras**

* There are 56 Yantras, each with its own miracle benefits.
* Some examples:
	+ Sri Yantra: for learning, wisdom, creativity, finance, beauty, and spiritual enrichment
	+ Mahalaxmi Yantra: for wealth
	+ Kanakdhara Yantra: for gold and wealth
	+ Gayatri Yantra: for energy
	+ Durga Beesa Yantra: to get rid of miscellaneous problems
	+ ... (total 56 Yantras listed)

**How to Use Yantras in Meditation**

* Create a holy space for meditation.
* Select a Yantra based on the energy you want to invoke.
* Place the Yantra in a table with a candle in front.
* Light the candle and focus on the flame for 7 minutes.
* Try not to blink and focus on invoking the energy of the Yantra.

**Miracle Benefits of Yantras**

* Each Yantra has its own miracle benefits, such as:
	+ Removing malefic effects of the evil
	+ Strengthening positive powers
	+ Increasing concentration and focus
	+ Strengthening the aura
	+ Etc.
