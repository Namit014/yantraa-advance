* A sacred geometric diagram that serves as the physical residence of a deity or cosmic energy.
* Each line is a mathematical representation of a specific divine frequency.
* Made primarily from copper, which Vedic tradition identifies as a conductor of divine energy.

**Why Choosing the Right Yantra Matters**

* Choosing the wrong yantra can create energetic interference rather than harmony.
