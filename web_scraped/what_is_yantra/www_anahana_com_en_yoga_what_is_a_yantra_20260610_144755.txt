**Yantra Structure:**

* Refers to the geometry of a yantra, which typically consists of the following elements:
	+ Bindu (central point)
	+ Shringara (creative energy that flows out from the Bindu)
	+ Chakra (the outermost part, representing different aspects of reality)
* Examples of shapes and symbols used in yantras:
	+ Squares
	+ Circles
	+ Triangles
	+ Lotuses
	+ Hexagrams
	+ Pentagrams
	+ Octagons

**How to Use a Yantra:**

* No single correct way to use a yantra, but here are a few tips:
	+ Find a comfortable place to sit or stand
	+ Close your eyes and take a few deep breaths
	+ Gaze at the yantra and allow your mind to focus on its shape and form
	+ Repeat a mantra, such as "Om Namah Shivaya"

**Benefits of Using a Yantra:**

* Potential benefits of using a yantra:
	+ Help focus the mind and still thoughts
	+ Channel the energy of the universe into the body
	+ Tools for meditation, mantra recitation, and prayer
	+ Help attract abundance, prosperity, well-being, and good luck
* Note: These benefits are based on personal experiences and are not scientifically proven.
