**Yantra**

* "Yan" means to regulate, and "Tra" means to protect.
* A device that regulates the energy dynamics of the worshipper, to protect it from decay, disintegration, and death.
* The worshipper must meditate on its mystical form before starting work on a yantra.
* There are various types of yantras, but the most sacred is Sri Chakra.
* Yantras can be made of gold, silver, and copper.

**Types of Yantras**

* Golden Yantra: for worldly influence
* Silver Yantra: for health and long life
* Copper Yantra: for wealth
* Yantra made of all three metals: secures all aspirations

**Other Types of Yantras**

* Permanent Yantra: fixed on the ground, called achana (immoveable).
* Small Yantras: inscribed on pieces of crystal or metals, worn as charms, called dharana yogya.
