*Heat* is a **1995 crime drama** directed by **Michael Mann**, starring **Al Pacino** and **Robert De Niro**. Rotten Tomatoes’ critics consensus says it is “an engrossing crime drama” with compelling performances and that it confirms Mann’s mastery of the genre.[2]

The film follows **Neil McCauley**, a master criminal planning one last heist, and **Lt. Vincent Hanna**, who is trying to track him down while dealing with turmoil in his own life. The two men develop a mutual respect even as they try to stop each other.[2]
