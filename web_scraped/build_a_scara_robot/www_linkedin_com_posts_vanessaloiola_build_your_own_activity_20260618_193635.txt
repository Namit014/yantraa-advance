# SCARA Robot – Technical Specifications

## Overview
A beginner-friendly, build-your-own SCARA robot project designed and documented by Vanessa Loiola.

## Key Technical Details

- **Type:** SCARA robot
- **Degrees of Freedom:** 4
- **Motors:** 4 × NEMA 17 stepper motors
- **Gripper:** 1 × servo motor (controls the gripper)
- **Controller:** Arduino
- **Build Method:** 3D printing (implied by project context)

## Project Scope
The project covers the full design, build, and programming of the SCARA robot using Arduino. It is intended as a learning platform for robotics, mechatronics, and 3D printing.

## Cost Reference
One commenter (Hans-Günther Nusseck) noted that a comparable SCARA robot's total parts cost is **under €100**.

## Additional Context
- The project is described as beginner-friendly and suitable for those passionate about robotics looking to expand skills in design and programming.
- SCARA kinematics are noted as a good teaching tool for forward and inverse kinematics, as the math is based on standard trigonometry.
