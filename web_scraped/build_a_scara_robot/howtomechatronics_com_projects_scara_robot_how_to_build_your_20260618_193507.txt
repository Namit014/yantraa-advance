Based on the provided text, here are the extracted technical specifications and facts regarding the Arduino based SCARA Robot:

*   **Design:** Developed by Dejan, the robot features 4 degrees of freedom (DOF).
*   **Actuation:**
    *   4x NEMA 17 stepper motors.
    *   1x Servo motor (MG996R) for the end effector/gripper.
*   **Control System:**
    *   Brain: Arduino UNO board.
    *   Drivers: CNC shield with four A4988 stepper drivers.
    *   Software: Custom Graphics User Interface (GUI) built using the Processing development environment.
*   **Kinematics & Operation:**
    *   Supports both Forward and Inverse Kinematics.
    *   Forward Kinematics: Manual joint movement via sliders to calculate X, Y, and Z end-effector positions.
    *   Inverse Kinematics: Automatic calculation of joint angles for a desired end-effector position.
    *   Automation: Capable of saving and executing stored movements in a loop.
    *   Adjustable speed and acceleration via the User Interface.
*   **Mechanical Design & 3D Printing:**
    *   Designed using 3DEXPERIENCE SOLIDWORKS.
    *   Most parts are 3D printable (PLA+ and normal PLA).
    *   GT2 pulleys are 3D printable and parametric (adjustable tooth count).
    *   Total print time: Approximately 120 hours at 60mm/s.
    *   Horizontal expansion setting: -0.1mm for dimensional accuracy.
*   **Gear Ratios:**
    *   Joint 1: 20:1 reduction ratio (two stages).
    *   Joint 2: 16:1 reduction ratio (two stages).
    *   Joint 3: 4:1 reduction ratio (single stage).
*   **Components & Dimensions:**
    *   **Z-Axis:** Driven by an 8mm lead screw.
    *   **Smooth Rods:** Four 10mm smooth rods (400mm length).
    *   **Linear Bearings:** Four 10mm linear ball bearings.
    *   **Belts:** GT2 belts (200mm and 300mm closed loops).
    *   **Bearings:**
        *   1x Thrust ball bearing (40x60x13mm).
        *   2x Thrust ball bearing (35x52x12mm).
        *   5x Radial ball bearing (8x22x7mm).
    *   **Fasteners:** Various M3, M4, and M5 bolts and nuts (including M4x55mm bolts and M8x45mm bolts).
*   **Physical Dimensions:**
    *   Height depends on smooth rod length (40cm in this build).
    *   Lead screw is 2cm shorter than smooth rods (38cm) or requires spacer nuts for the motor.
