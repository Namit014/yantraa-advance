**Polishing Robots:**

* Modern polishing robots are smart machines that use arms, tools, and sensors to polish metals and other materials.
* They are used in factories to make cars, planes, tools, and electronics.
* Advantages of robot polishing:
	+ Reduces human error
	+ Saves time
	+ Reduces mistakes
	+ Improves product quality
	+ Keeps workers safe from dust and hard work
	+ Can change speed and pressure on their own

**High-Accuracy Features:**

* Sensor Alignment: Finds the part and improves polishing (used in car factories)
* Pressure Control: Changes force to achieve a smooth finish (used in airplane factories)
* Vision Monitoring: Checks for scratches and reduces mistakes (used in electronics factories)
* Automated Programming: Guides the robot path for faster work (used in metal factories)

**Robotics Technology:**
