**Robot Building**

* Components needed:
	+ Arduino Uno (or other microcontroller)
	+ Continuous rotation servos
	+ Wheels
	+ Caster roller
	+ Small solderless breadboard
	+ Distance sensor
	+ Mini push-button switch
	+ 10kΩ resistor
	+ USB A to B cable
	+ Set of breakaway headers
	+ 6 x AA battery holder with 9V DC power jack
	+ Pack of jumper wires or 22-gauge hook-up wire
*Assembly steps:
	+ Align servos on battery pack
	+ Affix servos to battery pack
	+ Attach breadboard to battery pack
	+ Attach Arduino microcontroller to top of servos
	+ Put wheels on servos
	+ Attach caster to bottom of breadboard

**Wiring**

* Break off two 3-pin headers
* Insert headers into pins 1-3 and 6-8 on row E of breadboard
* Connect servo cables to headers
* Connect red jumper wires from pins C2 and C7 to red (positive) rail pins
* Connect black jumper wires from pins B1 and B6 to blue (ground) rail pins
* Connect white jumper wires from pins 12 and 13 on Arduino to A3 and A8
* Attach sensor to front of breadboard
* Connect jumper wires to sensor
* Connect sensor to Arduino
