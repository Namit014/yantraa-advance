**Robotic Polishing**

* Uses robot arms with force-controlled tooling to finish surfaces automatically.
* Consistent force and speed control.
* Increases abrasive life by 200-300%.
* Removes variation and ensures every part meets the required finish grade.

**Surface Finishes**

* Brushed Finish: creates a uniform linear grain on metal surfaces using belts or wheels.
* Grained Finish: produces a controlled, repeatable surface texture.
* Stainless Steel Polishing (Ra Finish): polishes stainless steel to a defined Ra value.
* Mirror Finish: removes micro-scratches and buffs the surface to a high-gloss, mirror-like reflection.

**Force Control**

* Uniform pressure is critical for polishing.
* Force compliance devices automatically adjust contact pressure as the robot moves over contours or varying surface angles.
* Maintains a consistent finish while protecting delicate materials.

**Tooling**

* Servo spindles and backstands deliver constant force and precision.
* Add force control: automatically adjusts contact pressure.
* Tooling for the right material and finish: balancing speed, torque, and surface control.

**Demonstration and Validation**

* Test before deploying: replicate the process, test polishing wheels and compounds, and measure finish consistency.
* Validate the desired finish is achieved from day one.

**Integration Partners**

* Work with certified integration partners who understand how to combine force control, abrasive selection, and motion programming to deliver production-ready polishing systems.

**Robotic Tooling for Polishing Automation**

* PushCorp FinishX™: a pre-engineered robotic polishing solution for metals, glass, and composite materials.
* Combinations of optimized servo spindles and compliance devices to deliver consistent mirror or brushed finishes with faster deployment and easier integration.

**Force Compliance Devices**

* Maintain precise, repeatable contact pressure across contoured or delicate surfaces.
* Ensure even material removal, reduce abrasive wear, and protect parts from over-polishing.

**Robotic Servo Spindles**
