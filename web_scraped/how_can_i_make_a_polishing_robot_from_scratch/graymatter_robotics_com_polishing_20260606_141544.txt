**Product Overview**

* Scan&Polish is an AI-powered polishing solution that scans and polishes at the push of a button.
* It is designed to augment the workforce, maximize capacity and quality, and is the only fully autonomous robotic solution in high-mix manufacturing.

**Key Features**

* FLEXIBLE: polishes various shapes, sizes, and materials, handles high-mix without retooling or programming.
* EASY TO USE: intuitively designed for shop floor workers to scan and polish at the push of a button.
* CONSISTENT: delivers consistent, high-quality finishes at unmatched speed and reliable performance all day.
* AUTOMATIC COMPOUND DISPENSER: applies polishing compounds with precision, adjusting flow and pattern based on material and polishing stage.
* AUTOMATIC TOOL CHANGER: automatically switches tools to approach flat surfaces with larger tool diameter and switches to a smaller tool to work on curved and intricate features.
* GENERATE UNIQUE MODELS: utilizes high-fidelity sensors to swiftly scan and model parts within minutes, creating a precise, unique working model for each component.

**Configurations**

* Single Arm & Rail: simplifies operations with a single rail or pedestal/plate, available in 4, 8, and 12 meters.
* Dual-Arm & Rails: maximizes productivity with a high-performance dual-arm and rails loading system, ideal for tackling larger projects.
* MOBILE ARM & RAIL: brings efficiency to a new level with a mobile configuration featuring a 4 meter rail for horizontal setups.

**Benefits**

* Reduces labor shortages, quality issues, and production slowdowns.
* Achieves cost savings and alleviates years of backlog.
* Free automation readiness assessment (valued at $500K) from the expert engineering team at no cost.
