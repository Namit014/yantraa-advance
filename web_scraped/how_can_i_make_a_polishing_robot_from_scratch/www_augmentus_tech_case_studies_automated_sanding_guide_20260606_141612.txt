**Robotic Sanding**

* Fully automated system using industrial robots with sanding end effectors
* Uses controlled force in sanding tasks and works at a constant speed to ensure uniform output
* Equipped with sensors and vision systems to monitor surface irregularities and align robot motion for an even finish

**Robotic Polishing**

* Process of polishing surfaces using robots to achieve first-class finishes
* Parts are polished using polishing end effectors and advanced sensors that apply the required amount of pressure
* Uses abrasives in multiple cycles and can be accompanied by vision systems to achieve consistency
