**Polishing Robots: Automating the Finishing Process**

* Polishing is a key step in improving part quality, safety, and presentation.
* Automation of polishing can increase throughput and part quality.
* Polishing robots are useful for various industries, including:
	+ Aerospace
	+ Automotive
	+ Metals
	+ Retail
	+ Glass

**Robot Types:**

* Six-Axis Robots:
	+ Excellent for polishing applications
	+ Best-in-class dexterity
	+ Can contort arms at all angles
	+ Suitable for polishing complex part surfaces
* SCARA Robots:
	+ Good for simple polishing applications
	+ Limited reach
	+ Struggle with complex part geometries
	+ Suitable for small, simple parts
* Cylindrical Robots:
	+ Less common choice
	+ Suitable for simple polishing tasks on vertical parts

**Integrating a Polishing Robot:**

* Considerations for integrating a polishing robot include:
	+ Knowing when to automate
	+ Choosing major components
	+ Sourcing tools and equipment
* Indicators for automating polishing include:
	+ Current bottlenecks at the finishing stage
	+ Requirements for increased consistency and performance
	+ Concerns for safety around the polishing line
