**Sanding and Polishing Robots**

* Collaborative robots (cobots) offer a flexible solution for polishing or sanding tasks.
* Cobots adjust position to achieve the desired force, making them suitable for tasks that require a specific, often delicate touch.
* They improve product safety by making surfaces easier to clean, reducing susceptibility to corrosion, increasing wear resistance, and enhancing adhesion for coatings.
* Cobots can increase production, streamline finishing processes, and enhance workplace safety.
