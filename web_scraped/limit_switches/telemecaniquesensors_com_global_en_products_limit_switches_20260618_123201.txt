Telemecanique Sensors offers a broad line of mechanical limit switches. They provide IEC, NEMA, safety, wireless, and battery-less options. The company has a history of limit switch innovation dating back to the early 1900s.

Limit switches are available in standard, heavy duty, compact, miniature, safety, and application-specific models. Wireless and battery-less limit switches convert electronic signals into internet data for documentation and analysis.

Telemecanique Sensors offers application-specific IEC limit switches designed for particular applications such as hoisting, mining, minerals & metals, or belt conveying. These are designed for easy product selection, installation, and maintenance and were formerly known as OsiSense.

NEMA limit switches have been available since 1924. The 9007 series is designed for harsh industrial environments and is offered in various options.

Telemecanique Sensors also provides a range of IEC limit switches, which have been in use for over 90 years. These are designed for robustness, reliability, and ease of product selection, installation, and maintenance. These IEC limit switches were formerly known as OsiSense.
