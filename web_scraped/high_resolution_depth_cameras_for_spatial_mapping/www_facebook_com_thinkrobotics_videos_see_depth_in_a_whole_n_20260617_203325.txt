The Orbbec Astra 2 is a structured light 3D camera for depth sensing, providing high-resolution depth data and full HD RGB output. It features a sensing range suitable for real-world environments, an integrated IMU, and multi-camera synchronization. The camera offers reliable point cloud, IR, and RGB data for real-time perception and is designed for applications in robotics, AI vision, and spatial mapping.
