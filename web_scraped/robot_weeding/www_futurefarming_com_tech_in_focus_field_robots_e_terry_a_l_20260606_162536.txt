**E-TERRY Lightweight Weeding Robot**

**Key Features:**

* 3-wheeled design with ultra-flexible chassis for adaptable row spacing and crop type
* Patented, modular drivetrain with three individual in-wheel hub motors for precise speed control, zero-turn maneuverability, and independent wheel steering
* Powered by interchangeable lithium-ion batteries, providing up to 11 hours of runtime with a full 6-module configuration
* Weighs approximately 500 kg, with a robust build and low ground pressure
* Track width is adjustable from 1.5 to 3.0 meters (5 to 10 ft) and height can be adapted to different crop stages
* Foldable chassis fits into a standard van for easy transport

**Technical Capabilities:**

* AI-based phenotyping system recognizes crops with an accuracy of over 95% and targets weeds with a precision of up to 5 mm
* Collects agronomic data, including weed density and biomass, for data-driven field management
* Navigation system combines RTK GPS with AI-based visual navigation using cameras to detect crop rows
* Safety features include dual LiDAR sensors for obstacle detection, zone-based human and animal monitoring, and real-time environment mapping, compliant with ISO 18497 safety standards

**Performance:**

* Can work at speeds of up to 1.5 km per hour and cover approximately 3 hectares in a 9-hour working day
* Transport speed is up to 3.5 km per hour

**Service Model:**

* Offered as a service at a rate of €1,000 per hectare, eliminating the need for upfront investment
