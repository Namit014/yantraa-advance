**Product**: Tertill Weeding Robot

**Features**:

* Autonomous maintenance of up to 200 square feet of garden space
* Minimal maintenance required
* Powered by sunlight, with weatherproof construction
* Can eliminate nearly all hoeing, hand weeding, and herbicide applications
* Can work in gardens surrounded by a 4-inch-high barrier with no gaps (e.g. wood, stone, brick, wire fence, or plastic)

**Dimensions and Weight**:

* Diameter: 8.6 inches (4.3 cm)
* Height: 4.5 inches (11.4 cm)
* Weight: 2.5 pounds (1.14 kg)

**Specifications**:

* Solar panel: 4.3 x 5.3 inches (10.9 x 13.5 cm)
* Motorized wheels: 4
* String-trimmer blade: replaceable, lasts about a month
* Lithium-ion battery: charged by sunlight, operates between 70-85% charge
* Operating cycle: 2-5 minutes of trimming, 20 minutes of recharging
* Total running time per day: 2 hours, adequate for about 200 square feet of garden space

**Economic Analysis**:

* Price: $349
* Labor savings: $15 per hour, the Tertill pays for itself in about a year and a half for gardeners who work in mild climates year-round
* Half-year gardeners would reap the payoff in three gardening seasons

**Recommendations**:

* Not for container gardens, flower beds, or other garden spaces without ample room to maneuver
* Not secured by a PIN or other theft deterrent, so it should only be used in secure gardens.
