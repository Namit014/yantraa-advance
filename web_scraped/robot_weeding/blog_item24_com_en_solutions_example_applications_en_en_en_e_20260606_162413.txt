**Rowesys (Robotic Weeding System) Specifications:**

* Weight: 300 kg
* Dimensions: 2100 x 940 x 1200 mm
* Speed: 0.2 m/s (while removing weeds), maximum speed: 1 m/s (3.6 km/h)
* Weighing and processing mechanism: small ploughs dip about 2 cm into the ground, separate weeds from roots, and leave ripped-out plants on the surface to dry out
* Sensors/Cameras:
	+ Front camera: identifies rows of sugar beet and positions the robot accordingly
	+ Vertical camera: recognizes individual sugar beet plants and weeds, and differentiates between the two
* Frame structure: made from aluminum profiles from item MB Building Kit System
* Electronics: enclosed in a futuristic, minimalist aluminum hull
* Wheels: attached to the frame structure
* Motors: attached to the frame structure

**Technical Details:**

* The weeding robot travels at 0.2 m/s while removing weeds
* The ploughshares are opened or closed depending on the information from the second camera
* The robot is fully autonomous once it reaches the actual field, but manual controls are required for navigation
* The internal electronics are enclosed in a futuristic, minimalist aluminum hull

**Item Profile Technology:**

* item profile technology was used in the frame structure of the weeding robot
* The flexibility and stability of the item profiles were beneficial for the project
* The item Engineeringtool saved time and made the ordering, design, and manufacturing process easier

**Automation and Sustainability:**

* The project is part of a focus on sustainable agriculture and the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)
* The University of Applied Sciences and Arts Northwestern Switzerland (HAWK-Friedrichshafen) is also involved in this focus area
