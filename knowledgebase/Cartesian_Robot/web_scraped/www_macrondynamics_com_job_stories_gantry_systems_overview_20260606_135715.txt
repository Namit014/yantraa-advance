**Gantry Robot Advantages:**

1. 3+ axis of movement (X, Y, Z) with scalable design
2. Suitable for light to heavy / hanging loads
3. Flexible and efficient due to linear axes scalability
4. Inexpensive

**Gantry Robot Disadvantages:**

1. Cannot vary reach into or around obstructions
2. Linear slides, belts, and rails are not easily sealed against the environment
3. Not freestanding; requires stand or frame or other mounting

**Gantry System Benefits:**

1. Can utilize an entire cubic work envelope (96% of space and size)
2. Flexible and efficient due to linear axes scalability

**Gantry Robot Applications:**

1. PC Board assembly
2. Dispensing
3. Spraying
4. Material handling
5. Palletizing
6. Pick and place
7. Water jet
8. Plate welding
9. Friction welding
10. Assembly
11. Packaging
12. Unitizing
13. Sorting
14. Scanning
15. Tray loading/unloading
16. Camera positioning
17. Inspection
18. Glass cutting
19. Printing plotting
20. Laser cutting
21. Flying knives
22. Fastening and screw driving

**Gantry Robot Characteristics:**

1. 3 axes of movement (X, Y, Z)
2. Coordinates defined as X, Y, and Z
3. Each axis is arranged at right angles to allow three degrees of motion
4. Can be easily scaled to larger proportions in all three axes
5. Designed for simplicity, low cost, and scalability
6. Includes a rectangular or cubic work envelope

**Benefits of Gantry Robots:**

1. Inexpensive compared to other more complex robots
2. Can be easily disassembled into component parts for maintenance
3. downtime is reduced and maintenance costs are kept to a minimum
4. Cartesian Coordinate Robot systems are highly desirable in manufacturing

**Note:**

* Cartesian/Gantry Robots are very accurate and repeatable
* Gantry robots are intuitive to program and easy to visualize
* Most importantly, Gantry robots are configurable and can be prepared to take on the challenges of damp, hazardous, and dirty environments.
