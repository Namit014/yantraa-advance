**Delta Robot Design**

* Type: Rotary delta robot design
* Actuates with 3 rotating joints (shoulders) with parallel linkage to the end effector (toolhead)
* Options for tool rotation through center linkage

**Mechanics**

* Kinematic work area: ~280x280x300 mm
