**Hydroponics:**

* Used for growing plants indoors
* Allowing for multiple levels of installation without carrying dirt into the house

**FarmBot and Jib Boom Arm:**

* Original FarmBot design not suitable for multi-level installation
* Swinging jib boom arm solution for moving robotic arm between different levels
* Used linear bearings on supported smooth rods for pole running from ground to another rail

**Robotic Arm:**

* Initially used a 3D printed robotic arm from Thingiverse (EEZYbotarm)
* Eventually replaced by a gantry mounted to the jib boom arm to provide X, Y, and Z movement

**System Components:**

* Camera mounted on robotic arm
* Grow light running on a schedule
* Universal tool for various tasks
* Seeding part
* Hydroponic liquid monitoring (EC, PH, and flow)
* NFT (nutrient film technique) rail
* Way to move grow lights around if needed
* Hook for picking up net cups for seed germination and growth

**Future Development:**

* Add second layer to the system for NFT rail
* Hook up seeding part
