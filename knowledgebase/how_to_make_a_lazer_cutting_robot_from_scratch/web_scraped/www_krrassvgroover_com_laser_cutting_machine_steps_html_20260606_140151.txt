**Laser Cutting Machine Protective Lens Selection:**

1. **Shape and Size Match**: Easy to understand.
2. **Materials**: K9, quartz, with multiple grades available for different laser powers and usage. Consider cost of use when choosing.
3. **Coatings**: Laser anti-reflection coating with different technical parameters for different use environments.

**Lens Cleaning and Reuse:**

1. **Lens Material**: ZnSe (zinc arsenic), relatively brittle and should not be dropped or collided with hard objects.
2. **Handling Requirements**: Gloves and special operating set required to handle the laser cutting machine to protect the lens from skin oil and other contaminants.
3. **Cleaning Methods**: Optical tweezers or vacuum tweezers recommended for smaller lenses.

**Pre-Use and Pre-Cleaning Inspection:**

1. **Inspection**: Check the protection of the lens of the laser cutting machine before use and before and after cleaning.
2. **Magnifying Equipment**: Often needed to use magnifying equipment when inspecting lenses.
3. **Illumination**: Use a relatively bright light to illuminate the optical surface and enhance the specular reflection intensity of surface contaminants and defects.
