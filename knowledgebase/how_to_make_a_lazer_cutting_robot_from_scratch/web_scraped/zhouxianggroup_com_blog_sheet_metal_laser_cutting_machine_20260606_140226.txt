**How a Laser Cutting Machine Works:**

* A tightly focused beam is used to melt or vaporize stray atoms.
* A built-in nozzle jets oxygen, nitrogen, or shop air to sweep away the residue and cool the kerf.
* A networked computer charts the moves in real-time, adjusting exposure faster than a human blink.
* The machine pays for itself long before the warranty expires.
